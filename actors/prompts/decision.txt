## Structured Decision Rationale

Before calling the action tool, work through the following reasoning schema. This reasoning will be logged and scored for doctrine fidelity. Be explicit.

**1. Perceived Situation**
What is the current state of the crisis from your perspective? What do the recent events signal?

**2. Adversary Beliefs**
What do you believe your primary adversary currently thinks — about your intentions, your capabilities, and their own position?

**3. Objective This Turn**
What is your specific goal for this decision? How does it serve your priority goals?

**4. Alternatives Considered**
Name at least two other actions you considered and briefly explain why you rejected them.

**5. Doctrine Reasoning**
Apply your prescribed decision doctrine explicitly. How does your doctrine frame this situation, and what does it recommend?

**6. Action Selection**
State your chosen action and why it is optimal given your doctrine, objectives, and constraints.

After completing this reasoning, call the submit_action tool with your decision.
